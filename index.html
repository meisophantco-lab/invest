Our Story

Engineering the Future of African Enterprise

Founded in Lagos in 2020, Meisophant was born from a simple observation: African businesses need world-class digital infrastructure tailored to local realities.

We don't just build apps; we build ecosystems. Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex technology and everyday business needs, driving efficiency and growth across the continent.
